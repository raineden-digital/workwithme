About Eden

A strategic partner behind the scenes.

The story behind the writing, operations, communication, and care I bring to every partnership.

My Story

Words, systems, and purpose.

I’ve always believed that words carry weight.

Long before titles like project manager, executive assistant, or digital marketer became part of my work, I was simply someone who loved to write. Writing was never just about putting words on a page. It was about understanding people; their thoughts, emotions, intentions, and the things they often struggled to say out loud.

“That love for writing became the foundation of how I work.”

I learned how to listen carefully, communicate clearly, and connect with people beyond surface-level conversations. It shaped the way I solve problems, manage relationships, and create experiences that feel personal rather than transactional.

And then I started as a Client Services Lead for a VA agency. That role opened my eyes to the real engine behind businesses: people, systems, expectations, and trust.

Working with different clients across industries gave me a front-row seat to how businesses operate behind the scenes. Every client had different goals, different challenges, and different approaches to working. It taught me how to adapt quickly, lead with clarity, and handle moving parts without losing sight of the bigger picture.

“That experience naturally led me into deeper operational and strategic roles.”

I then transitioned into social media marketing, where I learned how to turn communication into connection and visibility. I stepped further into project management, helping teams stay aligned, organized, and moving forward with purpose. I also grew into executive assistance, becoming the kind of support that leaders rely on, where I don’t just manage tasks, but create order, protect time, and help vision move faster.

What ties all of it together is still the same thing that started it all.

“Communication.”

Whether I’m writing copy, managing projects, supporting executives, or building systems behind the scenes, my work has always been about understanding people and helping things flow better because of it.

I help businesses move with intention and purpose because that’s how I roll, too.

“Great work is never just about getting things done.”

It’s about creating clarity, building trust, and making sure the right things happen the right way. I’m all for purposeful leadership, creating meaningful impact, and supporting businesses that want to make the world a better place.

Beyond work, I also volunteer in teaching Bible education, especially in places where access to it is not always easy. It reminds me that purpose is not only found in career success, but also in service, compassion, and meaningful human connection.

How about you? I want to know your story. What matters to you? What are you building? What are you hoping to create? Let’s talk.

How I Work

Proactive, organized, and dependable.

In a world where clients find virtual assistants disappearing without warning, missing responsibilities, or face the frustration of constant hiring and replacing, integrity and ethics matter even more. Trust should never feel uncertain.

Whether I’m managing projects, supporting leadership, or handling the moving parts behind the scenes, I believe professionalism begins with character. Because strong systems matter, but trust is what makes everything work. It is about showing up, being accountable, and creating confidence that the person behind the work can truly be counted on.

What Matters To Me

Ownership, communication, and consistency.

I believe great work starts long before the task itself. It begins with ownership, honest communication, and the consistency to follow through.

I value taking responsibility, not waiting to be told what to do, but understanding what needs to be done and handling it with care. Clear communication matters because misunderstandings cost time, trust, and momentum. And consistency is what turns good intentions into real results.

If my story resonates with yours, let’s talk about the support, structure, and communication your next chapter needs.
